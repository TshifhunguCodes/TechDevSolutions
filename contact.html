Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website project take?

Timeline depends on the package: Starter (1-2 weeks), Business (2-3 weeks), Premium (3-4 weeks). Complex projects may take longer.

Do you provide hosting?

We can recommend reliable hosting providers and assist with setup. We also offer optional monthly maintenance packages.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes! We often work with businesses to redesign and modernize their existing websites for better performance and user experience.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include design, development, responsive optimization, basic SEO, and deployment. See our Services page for full details.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance packages (R300-R600) for updates, security monitoring, and content changes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and EFT. We require a 50% deposit to start, with the balance due upon completion.
