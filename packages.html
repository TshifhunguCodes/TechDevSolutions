Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our website packages

How long does it take to build my website?

Our typical timeline is 1-2 weeks for Starter packages, 2-3 weeks for Business packages, and 3-4 weeks for E-commerce packages. Custom solutions may take longer depending on complexity.

Do you provide hosting and domain registration?

Yes, we can help you purchase a domain name and set up hosting. We also offer monthly hosting packages that include security updates, backups, and technical support.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes! All our websites include a user-friendly content management system (CMS) that allows you to easily update text, images, and add new pages without any technical knowledge.

What if I need changes after the website is completed?

We offer a 30-day period after launch for minor adjustments at no additional cost. After that, we provide ongoing support packages for updates and maintenance.

Do your websites work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All websites we create are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
